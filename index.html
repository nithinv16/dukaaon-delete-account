Account Deletion Request

How to Delete Your DukaaOn Account

We're sorry to see you go. To request deletion of your DukaaOn account and associated data, please follow one of these methods:

Option 1: Send an Email Request

Click the button below to automatically generate an email request:

Send Account Deletion Email

Note: If the button doesn't work, please manually send an email to admin@dukaaon.in with the subject "DukaaOn Account Deletion Request" and include your registered phone number and business name in the body.

Email copied to clipboard!

Option 2: Request Through the App

  1. Open the DukaaOn app
  2. Go to Profile
  3. Tap on "Settings"
  4. Select "Delete Account"
  5. Follow the on-screen instructions

What Happens When You Delete Your Account

Data We Delete:

  • Personal Information: Your name, contact details, and profile information
  • Business Information: Business name, address, and other shop details
  • User Preferences: Settings and preferences associated with your account
  • Device Information: Device identifiers linked to your account

Data Retention:

  • Transaction Records: Basic transaction records may be retained for legal and financial compliance purposes for up to 7 years as required by law
  • Anonymized Data: We may retain anonymized usage data that does not identify you personally
  • Legal Requirements: Information required to be kept by applicable laws and regulations

Processing Time:

Your account deletion request will be processed within 30 days. You'll receive a confirmation email once your account has been deleted.
